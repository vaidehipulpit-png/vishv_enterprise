Commitment to Industrial Standards

Industrial manpower requires the highest level of trust and adherence to legal frameworks. At Vishv Enterprise, we strictly follow the Contract Labour Act and Minimum Wages Act, ensuring that the rights of our workforce are protected, and that all operations are conducted under strict regulatory protocols.

Regulatory Adherence

Contract Labour Act (1970)

Full compliance with CLRA regulations, ensuring valid licenses for all contracted workforce deployment.

EPF & ESIC Compliance

100% adherence to Provident Fund and Employee State Insurance contributions for social security.

Factory Act & Safety

Strict implementation of safety norms as per the Factories Act, ensuring a hazard-free work environment.

Minimum Wages Act

We guarantee fair compensation, adhering strictly to state and central minimum wage notifications.

Ethics & Safety Protocols

Payroll Transparency

Direct bank transfers and transparent salary slips to ensure zero exploitation of workforce.

Zero Child Labor

Strict age verification protocols to ensure no under-aged individuals are employed in any capacity.

Anti-Harassment Policy

A zero-tolerance policy towards harassment, ensuring a safe and dignified workplace for all.
